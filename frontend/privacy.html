Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's exactly what we do (and don't do) with your data.

Last updated: June 13, 2026

01 Introduction

RoyaleForge is an open-source application developed for the Clash Royale community. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our platform.

02 Information We Collect

RoyaleForge is a local-first application. When you self-host the platform:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not use cookies or analytics
  • We do not store any data on remote servers
  • All deck data stays on your local machine

03 Third-Party Services

RoyaleForge uses the following third-party services:

  • RoyaleAPI CDN: Card images are loaded from RoyaleAPI's CDN. Your IP address is visible to them when loading images.
  • Google Fonts: Typography is served from Google Fonts.
  • Chart.js CDN: Chart library loaded from jsDelivr CDN.

04 Data Storage

All data used by RoyaleForge is stored locally on your machine:

  • Card and deck data is stored in CSV files
  • ML models are loaded into memory at runtime
  • No user accounts or login credentials are required
  • Logs are written locally to your log directory

05 Open Source Transparency

RoyaleForge is 100% open-source. You can verify every claim in this policy by inspecting the source code on GitHub. There are no hidden tracking scripts, data collection mechanisms, or analytics tools.

06 Children's Privacy

RoyaleForge does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. As we don't collect any data at all, this policy applies to all users.

07 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

08 Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
