Mission

Make pro analytics accessible

We believe every Clash Royale player deserves access to the same advanced analytics used by top tournament competitors. RoyaleForge brings cutting-edge machine learning to your fingertips — completely free.

From casual ladder climbers to esports professionals, our platform provides the insights you need to build winning decks, understand the meta, and make smarter strategic decisions.

AI Models

Four models, one mission

Trained on thousands of battles and decks to deliver accurate predictions.

Win Rate Predictor
Random Forest Regressor

Predicts the expected win rate of any deck based on card composition and meta data.

Deck Strength Scorer
Gradient Boosting

Scores deck strength on a 0-100 scale with letter grades from F to S.

Archetype Classifier
Random Forest Classifier

Classifies decks into archetypes: Beatdown, Control, Cycle, Siege, or Hog Cycle.

Similar Deck Finder
KNN + Cosine Similarity

Finds the most similar decks from our database using vector similarity algorithms.
